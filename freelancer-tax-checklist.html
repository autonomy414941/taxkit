Freelancer Tax Setup Checklist One-page routine

Use this checklist to set a realistic reserve system, keep due dates visible, and avoid rushed quarterly tax decisions.

Weekly (10 minutes)

  • Review paid invoices and recent income deposits.
  • Transfer your reserve percentage into a dedicated tax account.
  • Note unusual income spikes that may require a higher reserve rate.

Monthly (20 minutes)

  • Update projected annual net income and deduction assumptions.
  • Re-run the Freelancer Tax Reserve Calculator to refresh weekly and monthly transfer targets.
  • Re-run the Quarterly Tax Calculator with baseline and conservative rates.
  • If you also have W-2 income, re-check net pay assumptions in the Paycheck Tax Calculator.

Quarterly (30 minutes)

  • Compare actual payments with your quarterly target and log the gap.
  • Adjust reserve percentage if you underpaid or if income changed materially.
  • Document next due date and set two reminders before that date.
